### Tracking Tech & Cookies: What's the Deal? 🍪
We're all about using Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep tabs on activity on our Service and stash away some info. These tracking tools include beacons, tags, and scripts, all working to collect and track info, helping us level up and analyze our Service. The tech we might use includes:

-   **Cookies (or Browser Cookies)**: A Cookie is just a tiny file chillin' on your device. You can tell your browser to give all Cookies the cold shoulder or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But heads up: if you don't play ball with Cookies, some parts of our Service might be off-limits. Unless you've told your browser to ditch Cookies, our Service will likely be using them.
-   **Web Beacons**: Look, some parts of our Service and our emails might have these tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear GIFs, pixel tags, and single-pixel GIFs). These bad boys let the Company, for example, count users who've checked out those pages or opened an email, and they're used for other cool website stats (like seeing how popular a section is or making sure our systems and servers are all good to go).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies hang around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you're offline, while Session Cookies vanish into thin air as soon as you close your web browser.

We actually use both Session and Persistent Cookies for a few reasons, like:

-   **Strictly Necessary / Essential Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Session Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely vital for us to hand you the services available through the Website and to let you use some of its cool features. They're a big help in authenticating users and keeping a lid on fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you asked for just wouldn't happen, and we only use these Cookies to make sure you get those services.

-   **Cookies Policy / Acceptance Notification Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are solely for checking if users have given the green light to the use of Cookies on the Website.

-   **Functionality Cookies**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are what let us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website, like recalling your login deets or your language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a smoother, more personalized experience, so you don't have to re-enter your preferences every single time you hit up the Website.

For even more juicy details on the Cookies we use and your options concerning them, just swing by our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
